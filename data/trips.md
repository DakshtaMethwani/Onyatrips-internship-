# Travellers — Available Trips

## 1. Spiti Valley Explorer
- **Duration**: 8 days / 7 nights
- **Route**: Shimla → Narkanda → Nako → Tabo → Kaza → Chandratal → Manali
- **Price**: ₹18,500 per person (group of 6+), ₹22,000 for smaller groups
- **Best Season**: June to September
- **Highlights**: Chandratal Lake, Key Monastery, Hikkim (world's highest post office), Pin Valley National Park, Dhankar Monastery
- **Inclusions**: Accommodation (guesthouses/homestays), all meals, transport (SUV/tempo traveller), permits, guide
- **Exclusions**: Personal expenses, travel insurance, flights/train to Shimla

## 2. Manali–Leh Highway Adventure
- **Duration**: 10 days / 9 nights
- **Route**: Manali → Rohtang Pass → Jispa → Sarchu → Leh → Nubra Valley → Pangong Lake → Leh
- **Price**: ₹24,000 per person (group of 6+), ₹28,000 for smaller groups
- **Best Season**: July to September
- **Highlights**: Rohtang Pass, Baralacha La, Magnetic Hill, Nubra Valley dunes, Pangong Tso, Khardung La (one of the highest motorable passes)
- **Inclusions**: Accommodation, all meals, transport, permits (Inner Line Permit included), guide
- **Exclusions**: Personal expenses, travel insurance, flights to Manali/from Leh

## 3. Kasol & Kheerganga Trek
- **Duration**: 4 days / 3 nights
- **Route**: Delhi → Kasol → Kheerganga (trek) → Chalal → back to Delhi
- **Price**: ₹7,500 per person (group of 8+), ₹9,500 for smaller groups
- **Best Season**: March to June, September to November
- **Highlights**: Parvati River walks, natural hot springs at Kheerganga, Chalal forest trail, Israeli café culture in Kasol
- **Inclusions**: Transport (Delhi pickup/drop), accommodation (tents at Kheerganga, guesthouse in Kasol), meals on trek days, guide
- **Exclusions**: Personal expenses, meals in Kasol town

## 4. Chopta–Tungnath–Chandrashila Trek
- **Duration**: 5 days / 4 nights
- **Route**: Haridwar → Chopta → Tungnath Temple (3,680m) → Chandrashila Peak (4,130m) → Deoria Tal → Haridwar
- **Price**: ₹9,800 per person (group of 8+), ₹12,000 for smaller groups
- **Best Season**: April to June, October to November
- **Highlights**: Tungnath (highest Shiva temple in the world), 360° Himalayan views from Chandrashila, Deoria Tal reflection lake, bugyal (alpine meadows)
- **Inclusions**: Transport (Haridwar pickup/drop), accommodation (guesthouses/tents), all meals on trek, guide, first-aid kit
- **Exclusions**: Personal expenses, travel insurance

## 5. Bir Billing Paragliding Weekend
- **Duration**: 3 days / 2 nights
- **Route**: Delhi → Bir → Billing (takeoff point) → Bir Landing Zone → Delhi
- **Price**: ₹8,500 per person (includes one paragliding flight)
- **Best Season**: March to May, September to November
- **Highlights**: Tandem paragliding from Billing (1,900m), Tibetan colony and monasteries in Bir, Palpung Sherabling Monastery
- **Inclusions**: Transport (Delhi pickup/drop), accommodation, breakfast & dinner, one tandem paragliding flight, guide
- **Exclusions**: Lunch, additional flights (₹2,500/flight), personal expenses

## 6. Kedarnath Yatra
- **Duration**: 6 days / 5 nights
- **Route**: Haridwar → Guptkashi → Sonprayag → Gaurikund → Kedarnath Temple → back to Haridwar
- **Price**: ₹12,500 per person (group of 8+)
- **Best Season**: May to June, September to October (temple open dates vary yearly)
- **Highlights**: Kedarnath Temple darshan, Vasuki Tal side trek (optional), Gaurikund hot springs, Mandakini River views
- **Inclusions**: Transport, accommodation (guesthouses/dharamshalas), breakfast & dinner, guide, pony/palki booking assistance
- **Exclusions**: Temple offerings, personal expenses, helicopter tickets (if opted)
